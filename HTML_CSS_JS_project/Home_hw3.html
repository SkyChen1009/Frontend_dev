The most Bloody, Distorted Hardcore Music Festvial

The Metal Inferno Festival, a fiery celebration of heavy metal music, is set to ignite the stage once again. With a lineup of renowned metal bands, headbangers from all corners of the globe gather to revel in the thunderous riffs and electrifying performances. Brace yourself for a sonic inferno like no other!

ChthoniC

Hailing from Taiwan, Chthonic is a force to be reckoned with in the metal scene. Their unique blend of melodic black metal infused with traditional Taiwanese elements captivates audiences worldwide. With thought-provoking lyrics centered on mythology and human rights, Chthonic's powerful music transcends cultural boundaries, leaving a lasting impact on metal enthusiasts.

Crystal Lake

Crystal Lake, the Japanese powerhouse, is a relentless force in the world of metalcore. Known for their explosive energy and intricate musicianship, they have garnered international acclaim. With a seamless fusion of crushing breakdowns, soaring melodies, and heartfelt lyrics, Crystal Lake's music resonates with fans, leaving a lasting imprint on the genre.

Flesh Juicer

Flesh Juicer, the Taiwanese metal sensation, is a sonic assault on the senses. Combining elements of death metal and industrial music, their sound is heavy, aggressive, and relentless. With visceral lyrics exploring dark themes, Flesh Juicer's intense live performances and raw energy have earned them a devoted following in the underground metal scene.

Obsess

Obsess, the Taiwanese indie sensation, is making waves in the music scene. With their unique blend of dreamy melodies and introspective lyrics, they create a sonic journey that resonates with listeners. Obsess' captivating performances and heartfelt compositions showcase their artistry, earning them a dedicated following and recognition in the indie music community.

Fire EX.

Fire Ex, the Taiwanese indie band, is setting the music scene ablaze. Their high-energy performances and infectious blend of rock, punk, and pop have garnered a massive following. Fire Ex's anthemic songs, infused with witty lyrics and social commentary, ignite a sense of rebellion and empowerment, making them a force to be reckoned with.

Infernal Chaos

Infernal Chaos, the Taiwanese metal powerhouse, unleashes sonic mayhem upon the world. With blistering riffs, thunderous drums, and guttural vocals, their music plunges listeners into a dark and intense realm. Infernal Chaos' relentless energy and technical prowess command attention, solidifying their status as a formidable presence in the metal community.
